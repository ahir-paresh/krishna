1. Information We Collect

We are committed to transparency about the information we collect. Below is a detailed breakdown:

A. Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected)

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, OS version, device model, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: App features accessed, wallpapers viewed/downloaded, time spent in app, crash reports, and performance metrics
  • Network Information: IP address (anonymized), connection type (Wi-Fi/mobile), and general location (country/region level only)
  • Interaction Data: User interactions with UI elements, search queries, filter preferences, and feature usage patterns

B. Information Collected via Third-Party Services

Service Type of Data Purpose
Firebase Analytics Event data, session info, user properties, crash logs, performance metrics Understanding app usage patterns, identifying bugs, improving user experience
Google AdMob Ad impressions, interactions, clicks, device advertising ID (AAID/IDFA), demographics Serving personalized advertisements, measuring ad performance, revenue generation

C. Optional Information

  • User Feedback: If you contact us or provide feedback, we collect the content of your message
  • Content Reporting: When you flag or report inappropriate content, we collect details about the reported wallpaper
⚠️ Important Note: We do NOT collect: personal names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, location beyond country level, biometric data, or other personally identifiable information (PII) unless explicitly provided by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features, improve performance, and fix bugs
  • Analytics & Insights: Understanding user preferences, popular content, and feature usage through Firebase Analytics
  • Personalized Advertising: Serving relevant ads through Google AdMob based on usage patterns and interests
  • Content Moderation: Processing user reports and flags to maintain content quality and respect for religious themes
  • Safety & Security: Detecting fraud, preventing abuse, and maintaining app security
  • Legal Compliance: Fulfilling legal obligations and responding to lawful requests
  • Communication: Responding to user inquiries and providing customer support

We will NOT: Sell your data to third parties for marketing purposes, use your data for purposes not listed above without consent, or share personal information with unauthorized entities.

5. Religious Sensitivity & Content Respect

Our Religious Commitment

This application is dedicated to celebrating Hindu deities and spiritual traditions. We approach all content with profound respect and reverence.

Content Guidelines

  • Respectful Representation: All images of Hindu deities (Brahma, Vishnu, Shiva, Devi, and all other Devatas) are selected and displayed with utmost reverence
  • Sacred Tradition: We recognize the spiritual significance of Hindu gods and ensure content reflects authentic religious traditions
  • No Mockery or Disrespect: We strictly prohibit content that mocks, demeans, or disrespects any religious tradition or deity
  • Diversity of Hinduism: We celebrate the diversity within Hinduism, including different regional traditions, interpretations, and art styles
  • Educational Context: Where applicable, content includes information about deity significance and spiritual teachings

Community Reporting & Moderation

  • Flag Button: Each wallpaper includes a flag/report button for users to report inappropriate, disrespectful, or offensive content
  • Review Process: Our team reviews all flagged content within 48 hours
  • Removal Policy: Content found to violate our religious respect guidelines is immediately removed
  • User Feedback: We actively listen to community feedback regarding cultural sensitivity and make improvements accordingly

Our Non-Discrimination Statement

While this app focuses on Hindu religious themes, we respect all faiths and traditions. Our purpose is never to hurt or demean any religion. We celebrate the beauty of Hindu spirituality while respecting the beliefs of all users.

  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to data, and access is logged and monitored
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security assessments and updates

⚠️ Security Disclaimer: While we implement comprehensive security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we commit to using industry best practices.
